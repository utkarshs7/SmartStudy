What We Provide ?



Collaborative Notepad

You can write or save images of your regarding any topic and save it for sharing or for asking questions related to it within your study group. The text along with questions and answers can be saved in your personalized "Card Box" for future reference.


Question & Answers

  • Relevant questions can be attached with the image(s) sent to friends or teachers over the web for having personalized discussions through the replies that are received and sent till the time the discussion is closed. They chat and discuss simultaneously.

  • A Blind discussion involving individual(s) without any of them knowing about who all are being asked.


Card Box

This is your personalized notebook having asked question(s) and answer(s) from your discussion, for a quick glance whenever you want. It's safe and secure, the stored image along with the question and answers are visible to the user who asked the question.


Search

Search through the web to get relevant information, you can even save the links for quick reference. A special feature for the "one-nighters" who wan't to get the grasp of the related material in one go. We basically fetch the data from the most popular search engines and display the relevant links and notes in our custom user interface.
